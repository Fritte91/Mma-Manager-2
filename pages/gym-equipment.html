Gym Equipment in MMA Manager 2

Gym Equipment in MMA Manager 2

In MMA Manager 2: Ultimate Fight, outfitting your gym with the right equipment is essential for training fighters and enhancing their skills. Here's a comprehensive list of gym equipment along with brief descriptions of their functions and the attributes or skills they help develop:

Gym Equipment (Base Stat Training)

Treadmill

Improves fighters' conditioning, enhancing their stamina and endurance.

Bench Press

Builds strength, increasing a fighter's power and ability to deliver impactful strikes.

Stepper Board

Enhances agility, allowing fighters to move swiftly and react quickly during bouts.

Training Bike

Boosts conditioning, further developing cardiovascular fitness for prolonged fights.

Dumbbells

Focuses on strength training, aiding in muscle development for more powerful attacks.

Speed Bag

Improves agility and hand-eye coordination, essential for quick and precise strikes.

Fight Equipment (Skill Training)

Knee Bag

Trains knee strikes, enhancing the effectiveness and precision of knee attacks.

Free Standing Dummy

Develops clinchwork skills, allowing fighters to control opponents in close combat.

Punching Bag

Focuses on punches and elbow strikes, improving striking power and technique.

Kick Bag

Trains high and low kicks, boosting the speed and strength of leg strikes.

Grappling Dummy

Enhances ground grappling, submission techniques, and takedowns, crucial for ground fighting proficiency.

Rings (Defense Training)

Wooden Boxing Ring

Provides a space to train various defensive skills, including clinchwork, ground grappling, strike defense, knee defense, kick defense, takedown defense, and submission defense.

Homemade MMA Ring

Offers a versatile environment for comprehensive training, accommodating multiple aspects of both offensive and defensive techniques.

Each piece of equipment occupies a specific amount of floor space in your gym and can be upgraded to increase their effectiveness, such as enhancing training speed bonuses and maximum skill points. Investing in the appropriate equipment tailored to your fighters' needs is crucial for developing a well-rounded and formidable team.
